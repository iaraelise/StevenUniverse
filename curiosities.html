Rebecca Sugar

Rebecca Sugar is the creator of the animated series Steven Universe. While developing her own show, she also served as a writer for the animation Adventure Time; both were aired on Cartoon Network.


In addition to her work as an animator, she is also a dedicated musician, having composed several songs for both Adventure Time and Steven Universe. In November 2023, she released her first solo album, titled Spiral Bound.

Steven Sugar and His Influence

The look of the character Steven Universe was inspired by Rebecca Sugar's brother, Steven Sugar, who also served as the inspiration for the protagonist's name. Much like his sister, Steven is an animator and has worked on productions such as Ben 10. During the creation of Steven Universe, he contributed to the background design of the animated series.

Geek References

The animation is widely known for its geek culture references, being rich in pop culture elements with various tributes to anime and video games. One of the most talked-about references is the concept of fusion, clearly inspired by Dragon Ball. Additionally, the series alludes to other anime, such as Sailor Moon, and games like Final Fantasy.
