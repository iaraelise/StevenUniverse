Welcome to the magical universe of Steven

Steven Universe is an animation created by animator Rebecca Sugar, debuting in the United States on Cartoon Network in 2013. In Brazil, the series was also aired by Cartoon Network, premiering in 2014.


The animation tells the story of Steven and his guardians, Garnet, Pearl, and Amethyst. Together, they form the Crystal Gems, a group responsible for protecting the city of Beach City against Gem threats. The cartoon brings with it a wealth of self-knowledge, empathy, and sensitivity.
